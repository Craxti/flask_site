DACC (DAC)

This blockchain project is almost entirely content based and is a distributed file system. According to experts assessing the potential of startups, this allows for a kind of revolution in the digital media industry. Now the technology, which is called the next breakthrough in the blockchain industry, is already supported by a large number of companies. Crypto Capital, FBG Capital, TRON, Consensus Capital and some other projects participate in the financing. The capitalization of the currency is no more than 2 billion dollars, and the most active exchange on which it is placed is KuCoin.

What is the feature?

As conceived by the founders, the user will have full control over the creation of content, access to it, sending and storing early versions. The distinctive features of the currency, however, do not allow it to grow significantly; the value of the token falls from month to month.
